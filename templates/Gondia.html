Must-Visit Attractions

Sunset Point

Catch the beautiful sunset view at this serene location, perfect for nature lovers.

Purvanchal Forest

Explore the lush green forests and diverse wildlife of Purvanchal for a peaceful escape.

Devpur Caves

Discover the ancient rock-cut caves and learn about Gondia’s historical roots.

Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary

A haven for wildlife enthusiasts, home to a variety of flora and fauna.

Chandrapur Fort

A historical fort with breathtaking views and rich heritage, located near Gondia.

Rukmini Devi Temple

A beautiful temple by the Wainganga river, offering peace and spirituality.

Madan Mahal Fort

A historical fort offering spectacular views of Gondia and its surroundings.

Gondia Lake

A serene and peaceful lake, perfect for relaxing, picnics, and boat rides.

About Gondia

Gondia, located in the northeastern part of Maharashtra, is known for its picturesque landscapes, historical sites, and agricultural richness. Often referred to as the "Rice Bowl of Maharashtra," it offers a blend of rural charm and natural beauty, making it a peaceful retreat for those looking to experience the real essence of Maharashtra.
