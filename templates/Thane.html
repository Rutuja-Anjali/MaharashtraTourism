Must-Visit Attractions

Upvan Lake

Beautiful lake surrounded by hills and perfect for a peaceful evening walk.

Masunda Lake

A serene lake with boating facilities and a lovely promenade for relaxation.

Yeoor Hills

A beautiful hilly region, ideal for nature walks and outdoor activities.

Kalwa Fort

A historic fort offering stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

Tikuji-ni-Wadi

An amusement park with water rides, a toy train, and a mini zoo.

Central Park

A large, well-maintained park with lush greenery, walking paths, and recreational areas, perfect for a peaceful escape.

About Thane

Thane, located in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, is known for its scenic lakes, beautiful gardens, and rich historical landmarks. The city is often referred to as the "City of Lakes" due to its many lakes, such as Upvan Lake and Masunda Lake. With historical forts, temples, and lush greenery, Thane offers a perfect blend of urban and rural experiences.
