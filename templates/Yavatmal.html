Must-Visit Attractions

Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary

A lush haven for wildlife enthusiasts, home to tigers, leopards, and diverse flora.

Arunawati Dam and Reservoir

A serene spot perfect for nature lovers and peaceful escapes.

Kalamb Temple

An ancient Ganesha temple offering spiritual solace in a tranquil setting.

Waghadi River

A picturesque river ideal for relaxation and scenic views.

Bembla Dam

A serene dam surrounded by nature, perfect for picnics and quiet retreats.

Jagat Mandir

A beautifully crafted temple with a divine ambiance, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike.

About Yavatmal

Yavatmal, nestled in nature’s lap, offers scenic beauty with places like Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary and Arunawati Dam, alongside spiritual havens like Kalamb Temple and Jagat Mandir, making it an ideal blend of tranquility and adventure.
