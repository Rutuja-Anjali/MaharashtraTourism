Local Cuisine

Puran Poli

A famous Maharashtrian sweet flatbread stuffed with jaggery and lentils.

Zunka Bhakri

A spicy gram flour dish served with Bhakri, a staple rural meal.

Kothimbir Vadi

A crispy, savory snack made from coriander leaves, gram flour, and spices.

Misal Pav

A spicy curry made of sprouted moth beans, served with pav and farsan.

Sabudana Khichdi

A light and flavorful dish made with tapioca pearls, peanuts, and potatoes, often eaten during fasting.

Sol Kadhi

A refreshing drink made with coconut milk and kokum, served as a digestive.

Modak

A sweet dumpling filled with jaggery and coconut, a favorite of Lord Ganesha.

Thalipeeth

A multigrain savory flatbread, typically served with butter or yogurt.

Pithla Bhakri

A rural staple made of gram flour curry (pithla) served with jowar bhakri.

Pav Bhaji

A popular street food of Mumbai, consisting of mashed spicy vegetables served with buttered pav.

Kolhapuri Chicken

A fiery, spicy chicken curry from Kolhapur, known for its bold flavors.
