Must-Visit Attractions

Deekshabhoomi

Deekshabhoomi is a significant religious monument where Dr. B.R. Ambedkar embraced Buddhism. It is a symbol of the Dalit Buddhist movement.

Dragon Palace Temple

A Buddhist temple that blends Chinese and Japanese architecture. It is a peaceful place to meditate and enjoy the serenity of the surroundings.

Nagpur Central Museum

The Nagpur Central Museum showcases a vast collection of artifacts, sculptures, and historical exhibits.

Maharaj Baug and Zoo

This historical garden and zoo is a great place for nature lovers and families.

Sitabuldi Fort

Sitabuldi Fort is a historical fort that stands on a hill in the center of the city. It is significant for its role during the British era and offers views of the city.

About Nagpur

Nagpur, known as the "Orange City" of India, is renowned for its trade in oranges, as well as its rich cultural heritage. It is the second capital of Maharashtra and is well connected by air, rail, and road. The city is a blend of ancient history and modern development. From religious sites like Deekshabhoomi to the serene Futala Lake, Nagpur offers an array of experiences for visitors.
