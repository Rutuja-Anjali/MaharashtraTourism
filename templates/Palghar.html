About Palghar

Palghar, located in the northern part of Maharashtra, is a hidden gem known for its pristine beaches, tranquil dams, and rich historical sites. From the beautiful Kelva Beach to the serene Vaitarna Dam, Palghar offers a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle of the city. The district is also home to several forts, temples, and wildlife sanctuaries that add to its charm.
