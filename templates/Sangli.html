Must-Visit Attractions

Sangli Fort

A historical fort offering a glimpse into Sangli's past, surrounded by scenic views.

Sagareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary

A beautiful wildlife sanctuary located near Sangli, home to various animals, birds, and lush greenery.

Dandoba Hills Forest Reserve

Spend the day amidst wildlife and lush greenery at the Dandoba Hills Forest Reserve, known for its diverse flora and fauna.

Krishna River

The river that flows through Sangli, offering scenic beauty and activities like boating and fishing.

Narsobachi Wadi

A famous pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Narsimha, situated on the banks of the Krishna River.

About Sangli

Sangli, located in southwestern Maharashtra, is known for its temples, forts, and scenic landscapes. The district is famous for its agricultural produce, particularly grapes and pomegranates. The region boasts of numerous historical landmarks, making it a significant tourist destination in the state.
