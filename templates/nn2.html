Must-Visit Attractions

Mehrun Lake

A serene retreat in Jalgaon, perfect for peaceful evenings by the water.

Manudevi Waterfall

A breathtaking cascade nestled in lush greenery, a nature lover's paradise.

Patnadevi Lake

An ancient temple steeped in history, dedicated to Goddess Patnadevi.

Gandhi teerth

A world-class museum celebrating the life and teachings of Mahatma Gandhi.

Swinging Towers Of Farkande

Ancient towers that sway but never fall, a true architectural marvel.

Shree Padmalaya Temple

A sacred Ganesha temple with unique self-manifested idols, radiating spirituality.

About Jalgaon

Jalgaon, known as the 'Banana City of India,' is a delightful blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. From the serene Mehrun Lake to the majestic Manudevi Waterfall and iconic sites like Gandhi Teerth and Patnadevi Temple, the district offers a rich tapestry of experiences. Its lush landscapes, ancient marvels, and spiritual ambiance make Jalgaon a must-visit destination for every traveler.
