Must-Visit Attractions

Ahmednagar Fort

The historic fort built in the 15th century, known for its role in Maratha history and its impressive architecture.

Meherabad

The spiritual center of Meher Baba, with his tomb and a peaceful atmosphere that attracts pilgrims worldwide.

Ranjangaon Ganpati Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located in a serene environment that attracts numerous devotees.

Umbrella Falls

Located near Bhandardara, Umbrella Falls is a scenic spot that offers a beautiful monsoon view with ca2scading water forming an umbrella-like structure.

Salabat Khan's Tomb

Salabat Khan's Tomb is a significant historical site in Ahmednagar, known for its Islamic architectural style. The tomb is dedicated to Salabat Khan, a prominent figure in the Mughal Empire.

Farah Bagh

Farah Bagh is a historical garden built during the reign of the Mughal Empire, situated near Ahmednagar. It is famous for its beautiful surroundings and well-maintained architecture.

About Ahmednagar

Ahmednagar, known for its historical significance, is home to several forts, temples, and natural beauty. From the peaceful Meherabad to the picturesque Umbrella Falls, this district offers a variety of experiences for travelers. The tomb of Salabat Khan and other historical monuments also provide a glimpse into the region's rich cultural heritage.
