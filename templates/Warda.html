About Thane

Nestled in the heart of Maharashtra, Wardha is a town rich in history and culture. Famous for its connection to Mahatma Gandhi’s freedom struggle, it is home to the iconic Gandhi Ashram and the serene surroundings of Sevagram. With its peaceful ambiance, historical significance, and spiritual heritage, Wardha offers a unique glimpse into India's fight for independence and its enduring values.
