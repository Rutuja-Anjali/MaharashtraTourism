Must-Visit Attractions

Shaniwar Wada

A historic fortification and one of Pune's most iconic landmarks.

Lal Mahal

A historic palace built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in the 17th century.

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple

A famous Ganesh temple in Pune, rich in history and devotion.

Sinhagad Fort

A scenic hilltop fort with breathtaking views and historical significance.

Pataleshwar Cave Temple

An ancient rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, famous for its architecture.

Aga Khan Palace

A significant historical site, where Mahatma Gandhi was imprisoned during the freedom struggle.

Vishrambaug Wada

A historical mansion with rich cultural and architectural value.

Osho Ashram

A peaceful meditation retreat attracting seekers from around the world.

About Pune

Pune, known as the cultural capital of Maharashtra, is a vibrant city blending the old and the new. It has a rich history, educational prominence, and scenic beauty. From historical sites like Shaniwar Wada to peaceful hill stations, Pune has a lot to offer to tourists.
