Must-Visit Attractions

Sindhudurg Fort

A historic fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, located on an island in the Arabian Sea.

Devbagh Beach

A serene and less crowded beach perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Tarkarli Beach

Famous for its clean sand, clear waters, and a great spot for scuba diving and snorkeling.

Vijaydurg Fort

A historical fort located on the shores of the Arabian Sea, known for its impressive architecture and views.

Khavane Waterfall

A picturesque waterfall near Malvan, surrounded by lush greenery and a peaceful environment.

About Sindhudurg

Sindhudurg is a coastal district located in the southwestern part of Maharashtra, India. It is famous for its pristine beaches, historic forts, and rich cultural heritage. The district's name is derived from the word "Sindhu," meaning "ocean," and "Durga," meaning "fort," which refers to the iconic Sindhudurg Fort. With a vast coastline along the Arabian Sea, Sindhudurg is a hub for water sports, nature lovers, and history enthusiasts. The district's serene landscapes, tropical climate, and laid-back atmosphere make it an ideal getaway for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat.
