Must-Visit Attractions

Pranhita Wildlife Sanctuary

A peaceful sanctuary at the confluence of the Pranhita and Godavari rivers, perfect for wildlife lovers and nature enthusiasts.

Abujhmar Hills

Rugged mountain range perfect for trekkers, photographers, and wildlife enthusiasts.

Rani Durgavati Mahal

A historical palace associated with Queen Rani Durgavati, reflecting the grandeur of the region's royal history.

Sironcha Fort

A fort with historical significance, offering great views of the surrounding landscapes.

About Gadchiroli

Gadchiroli is a scenic district located in the southeastern part of Maharashtra, India. The district is known for its rich biodiversity, untouched forests, and beautiful landscapes. Surrounded by the majestic Abujhmar hills, Gadchiroli is home to several wildlife sanctuaries, waterfalls, and historical sites. It is also known for its indigenous tribal culture, with many tribal communities residing in the area. The district offers an ideal blend of nature, history, and cultural heritage, making it a hidden gem for travelers looking for an offbeat experience.
