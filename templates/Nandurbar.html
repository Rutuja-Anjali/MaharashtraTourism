About Nandurbar

Nandurbar, located in the northwestern part of Maharashtra, is a district rich in natural beauty, cultural heritage, and historical landmarks. It is renowned for its temples, forts, and the serene Narmada River. The district offers a peaceful escape for travelers, with attractions like Devgarh Fort, Saptashrungi Temple, and Amba Gad, where visitors can enjoy the scenic beauty and delve into local history. Nandurbar is also known for its agricultural richness and vibrant cultural traditions, making it a hidden gem in Maharashtra.
