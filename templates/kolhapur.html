Must-Visit Attractions

Mahalakshmi Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, attracting devotees from all over India.

Panhala Fort

Historic fort with scenic views and a rich history, known for its strategic location on Panhala Hill.

Rankala Lake

A serene and peaceful lake offering boating opportunities and picturesque views of the surroundings.

Shivaji University

A prominent educational institution with a beautiful campus surrounded by lush greenery.

New Palace (Raja's Palace)

A magnificent royal palace now turned into a museum showcasing the region’s royal history.

Kopeshwar Temple

An ancient Shiva temple with intricate carvings, reflecting the architectural brilliance of the time.

About Kolhapur

Kolhapur, located in the southern part of Maharashtra, is a city steeped in cultural heritage and history. It is famous for its temples, forts, and beautiful lakes. The city has been a historical center of Maratha culture and politics, and it remains an important destination for religious and historical tourism. Kolhapur is also known for its unique handicrafts, especially Kolhapuri chappals, and its delicious cuisine.
