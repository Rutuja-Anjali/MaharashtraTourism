Must-Visit Attractions

Nandur Madhmeshwar Bird Sanctuary

A haven for birdwatchers, this sanctuary is home to various migratory and resident bird species. A must-visit for nature lovers!

Shree Saptashrungi Gad

A sacred hill temple dedicated to Goddess Saptashrungi, offering stunning views after a rewarding trek.

Panchavati

A sacred place with religious significance, believed to be where Lord Ram spent time during his exile. It's rich in history and spiritual ambiance.

Waghur Dam

Visit this picturesque dam near the Waghur River, perfect for photography, boating, and relaxing amidst nature.

Laling Fort

Discover the historical Laling Fort, offering stunning views and a rich history waiting to be explored.

About Nandurbar

Dhule is a vibrant city in northern Maharashtra, known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and growing industrial and agricultural sectors. Serving as the administrative headquarters of the Dhule district, it is an important transit hub on the Mumbai-Northern India route. The city's economy is bolstered by industries like textiles, food processing, and engineering, alongside a strong agricultural base with crops such as cotton, wheat, and sugarcane. With a range of educational institutions and a favorable climate, Dhule is emerging as a key development center in the region.
