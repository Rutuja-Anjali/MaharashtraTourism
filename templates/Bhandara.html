Must-Visit Attractions

Vishnu Temple

A historic and sacred temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, offering spiritual solace.

Khindsi Lake

A serene lake offering boating and picnicking opportunities amidst lush greenery.

Gonhi Waterfall

One of Bhandara’s most beautiful natural wonders, perfect for trekking and nature lovers.

Tomb of Nawab Shamsher Khan

A historic tomb reflecting the Maratha and Mughal influences in Bhandara.

Anandvan

A retreat center known for its peaceful surroundings and social work, offering a spiritual atmosphere.

About Bhandara

Bhandara, located in the northeastern part of Maharashtra, is known for its scenic beauty, temples, and waterfalls. The district is rich in culture and history, with numerous temples that reflect the deep religious roots of the region. Bhandara's lush landscapes, lakes, and waterfalls make it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.
