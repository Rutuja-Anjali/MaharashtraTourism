Must-Visit Attractions

Gugamal National Park

A beautiful sanctuary for wildlife enthusiasts, featuring dense forests and a variety of flora and fauna.

Rishikesh waterfall

A serene waterfall surrounded by natural beauty, perfect for a peaceful retreat.

Shri Ganesh Temple

Known as "Rajur Ganpati," this ancient temple is a revered religious site dedicated to Lord Ganesha.

Jalna fort

A historic landmark showcasing the region's rich history and architectural heritage.

Anandi Swami Temple

Known for its spiritual vibes and peaceful environment.

Bhokardan Buddhist Tukai Caves

Step into the mystical Tukai Caves and feel the echoes of ancient meditation.

About Jalna

Jalna, a vibrant city in Maharashtra, is a treasure trove of history, spirituality, and natural beauty. Famous for its ancient temples like Rajur Ganesh and Matsyodari Devi, the historic Jalna Fort, it offers a perfect blend of heritage and tranquility. A must-visit destination for culture and peace seekers!
