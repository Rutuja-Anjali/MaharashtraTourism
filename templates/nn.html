Must-Visit Attractions

Ajinkyatara Fort

A historic hilltop fort offering a panoramic view of Satara city and its surrounding valleys.

Pratapgad Fort

Where history meets the mountains—an iconic Maratha fort with stunning views and rich heritage.

Kaas plateau

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the 'Valley of Flowers' in full bloom is a nature lover's paradise.

Thoseghar Waterfall

Majestic, high waterfalls nestled in a serene environment, perfect for a nature escape.

Mahabaleshwar

A charming hill station known for its cool climate, scenic spots, and beautiful landscapes.

Lodwick Point

A stunning viewpoint that offers breathtaking vistas of the valleys and hills of Mahabaleshwar.

About Satara

Satara, located in the heart of Maharashtra, is a scenic destination known for its historical significance and natural beauty. Surrounded by lush hills, majestic forts like Pratapgad and Ajinkyatara, and vibrant spots like the Kaas Plateau and Thoseghar Waterfall, Satara offers a perfect mix of adventure and tranquility. Whether you're exploring the rich Maratha history or enjoying the breathtaking landscapes, Satara promises an unforgettable experience.
