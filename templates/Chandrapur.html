About Chandrapur

Chandrapur, located in the eastern part of Maharashtra, is known as the "City of Black Gold" due to its rich coal deposits. The district is famous for its lush forests, diverse wildlife, and ancient temples. One of the major attractions is the Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve, which is one of the oldest and largest national parks in Maharashtra, home to a significant population of tigers. Chandrapur also boasts historic landmarks like the Chandrapur Fort and the famous Mahakali Temple, offering a blend of nature, wildlife, and history for all visitors.
