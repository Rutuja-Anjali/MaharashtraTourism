About Buldhana

Buldhana, a charming district in Maharashtra, is a blend of natural wonders, spiritual heritage, and historical treasures.ancient temples like Shri Gajanan Maharaj Temple in Shegaon, and serene spots like Anand Sagar, it offers a peaceful escape for travelers. With its rich history and tranquil landscapes, Buldhana is a perfect destination for nature lovers, pilgrims, and history enthusiasts alike.
