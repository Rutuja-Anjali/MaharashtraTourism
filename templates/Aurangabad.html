Must-Visit Attractions

Ajanta Caves

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves, famous for their exquisite sculptures and murals, dating back to the 2nd century BC.

Bibi Ka Maqbara

Known as the "Taj of the Deccan," this beautiful tomb was built by Azam Shah in memory of his mother.

Daulatabad Fort

An ancient hill fort with a fascinating history and impressive strategic positioning.

Grishneshwar Temple

A beautiful shrine and a popular spot to experience the religious diversity of Aurangabad.

panchakki

A historic water mill that demonstrates the architectural brilliance of the Mughal era.

About Aurangabad

Aurangabad, located in Maharashtra, is a city that blends rich history with stunning architectural beauty. Known as the "City of Gates," it is home to several ancient monuments and historic sites, including the famous Ajanta and Ellora Caves, which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Aurangabad is also renowned for its Mughal architecture, with iconic landmarks like the Bibi Ka Maqbara and Aurangabad Fort. The city offers a unique glimpse into India's past, with its forts, temples, and gardens, making it a must-visit destination for history and culture enthusiasts.
