Must-Visit Attractions

Kalaram Sansthan

A spiritual destination, Kalaram Sansthan is a prominent temple dedicated to Lord Rama.

Shirdi Sai Baba Temple

One of the most famous pilgrimage destinations in India, the temple of Sai Baba draws millions of devotees every year.

Saptashrungi Temple

A beautiful temple nestled on a hilltop, dedicated to Goddess Saptashrungi, offering panoramic views of the region.

Trimbakeshwar Temple

A sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its historical and religious significance.

Gangapur Dam

Known for its serene surroundings, this dam offers a peaceful retreat and excellent opportunities for photography.

Pandav Leni Caves

A group of ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves, offering a peaceful retreat and an insight into India's Buddhist heritage.

About Nashik

Nashik, a city in Maharashtra, is one of the holiest places in India, often referred to as the "Banaras of the South". Famous for its temples, vineyards, and natural beauty, Nashik is home to the renowned Trimbakeshwar and Kalaram temples. It also hosts the Kumbh Mela, a major pilgrimage event held once every 12 years. Nashik is also known for its proximity to the Saptashrungi Temple, the famous vineyards, and the scenic spots along the Godavari River. Whether you seek spiritual solace, adventure, or relaxation, Nashik offers something for everyone.
