Must-Visit Attractions

Solapur Fort

A historic fort known for its impressive architecture and its connection to Maratha history.

Pandharpur

A major pilgrimage town, famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple, one of the most revered temples in Maharashtra.

Bhuikot Fort

A 16th-century fort offering stunning views of the city and holding historical significance.

Nannaj Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for birdwatchers, the sanctuary is home to numerous migratory bird species.

Akkalkot Swaminarayan Temple

A prominent temple dedicated to Swaminarayan, located in Akkalkot near Solapur.

Siddheshwar Temple

A peaceful temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located near Siddheshwar Lake.

Ujani Dam

A major reservoir offering stunning views and recreational activities like boating and fishing.

About Solapur

Solapur, located in the southern part of Maharashtra, is a city with rich cultural, religious, and historical significance. Known for its historic forts, temples, and natural beauty, Solapur attracts tourists who seek both spiritual and historical experiences. The region is famous for its textiles, especially Solapur chaddars (blankets), and is a key player in the state's agricultural sector.
